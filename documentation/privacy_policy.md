# Privacy Policy for Egyptian Agent

Last updated: [DATE]

## Information Collection and Use

The Egyptian Agent app collects the following information to provide its services:

### Voice Data
- Voice commands are processed locally on your device
- No voice recordings are sent to external servers
- Voice data is not stored permanently on your device

### Contact Information
- Access to contacts is used to make calls and send messages
- Contact information is not shared with external services
- Contact data is only accessed when needed for specific commands

### Call Logs
- Access to call logs is used to read missed calls
- Call log data is not stored or shared externally
- Only read access is used for reading missed calls

### Sensor Data
- Accelerometer data is used for fall detection
- Sensor data is processed locally
- No sensor data is stored or transmitted

## Data Security

We prioritize the security of your personal information:
- All processing happens offline on your device
- No personal data is transmitted to external servers
- Local data is encrypted where possible
- Minimal data access is used for functionality

## Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:
- RECORD_AUDIO: For voice command recognition
- CALL_PHONE: To make phone calls
- READ_CONTACTS: To access contact names and numbers
- BODY_SENSORS: For fall detection using accelerometer
- SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: For overlay functionality
- WAKE_LOCK: To maintain operation in background

## Children's Privacy

The Egyptian Agent does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
